How credit repair works

Challenge
We will challenge questionable and inaccurate negative items reported by all of the bureaus which will ensure that your reports are fair and accurate.
Dispute
We insist that the creditors verify each and every negative item they report. If they are unable to verify them, they are bound by the law to stop reporting these.
Monitor
We will continuously monitor your credits and address any additional problems as they arise from time to time. This will allow you to stay on the track and attain your credit goals.
